The National Institutes of Health has released a budget to support research and progress in earlier diagnosis and treatment options for Alzheimer's and Dementia. The Alzheimer's Association provides resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, including educational programs, virtual events, and an online community. They also offer research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research, as well as opportunities for advocacy and philanthropy. The organization partners with various corporations and sponsors to support their mission.
