The Alzheimer's Association provides information, resources, and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, including a 24/7 helpline, educational programs, and an online community. They also emphasize the importance of brain health and offer resources specific to different communities. The organization offers research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research, as well as opportunities for advocacy and philanthropy. They partner with various corporations and sponsors to support their mission.
