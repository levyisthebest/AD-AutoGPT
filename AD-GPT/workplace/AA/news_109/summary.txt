The Alzheimer's Association provides information, resources, and support for individuals and families affected by Alzheimer's disease and related dementias. They offer a 24/7 helpline, educational programs, virtual events, and an online community for support. The organization also emphasizes the importance of brain health and offers tips for maintaining a healthy brain. Additionally, they provide research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research, as well as opportunities for advocacy and philanthropy.
