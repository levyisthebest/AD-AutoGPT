The House Appropriations Subcommittee has advanced funding for Alzheimer's and dementia research and support. The Alzheimer's Association provides resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, covering topics such as diagnosis, treatments, legal and financial planning, caregiving, safety, and research. They also offer educational programs, virtual events, an online community for support, and resources specific to different communities. The organization promotes brain health and offers research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research. They partner with various corporations and sponsors to support their mission.
