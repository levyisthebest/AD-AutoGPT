The Human Rights Campaign and Alzheimer's Association have partnered to raise awareness and support for those affected by Alzheimer's disease, with a focus on the LGBTQ+ community. The Alzheimer's Association provides resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, including educational programs, virtual events, and an online community. They also offer research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research, as well as opportunities for advocacy and philanthropy.
