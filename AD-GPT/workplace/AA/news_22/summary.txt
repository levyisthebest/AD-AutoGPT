The Alzheimer's Association provides resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, including diagnosis, treatments, legal and financial planning, caregiving, safety, and research. They also offer educational programs, virtual events, and an online community for support, as well as resources specific to different communities. The organization emphasizes the importance of brain health and offers tips for maintaining a healthy brain. They also offer research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research, as well as opportunities for advocacy and philanthropy.
