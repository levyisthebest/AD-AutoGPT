The Landmark U.S. Pointer Study has recruited over 2,000 participants, including 30% from underrepresented communities, to focus on early detection and prevention of Alzheimer's disease. The Alzheimer's Association provides resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, including educational programs, virtual events, and an online community. They also offer resources specific to different communities and emphasize the importance of brain health. The organization offers research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research, as well as opportunities for advocacy and philanthropy.
