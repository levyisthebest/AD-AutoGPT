The Alzheimer's Association provides a 24/7 helpline and website resources for information and support related to Alzheimer's and dementia, including diagnosis, treatment options, research, and support for caregivers. They also offer educational programs, virtual events, and an online community for support, as well as resources specific to different communities. The organization emphasizes the importance of brain health and offers research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research. They partner with various corporations and sponsors to support their mission.
