The Alzheimer's Association provides resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, including information on diagnosis, treatments, legal and financial planning, caregiving, safety, and research. They also offer educational programs, virtual events, and an online community for support, promote brain health, and provide information on how to stay physically, mentally, and socially active. The organization is committed to research and progress in the diagnosis and treatment of Alzheimer's disease, offering clinical trials and funding opportunities for professional researchers, as well as partnerships and advocacy programs. They also provide resources for those looking to donate or volunteer, and offer support for people living with dementia and their families.
