The Alzheimer's Association and MATTER have launched a pitch competition to find innovative solutions for Alzheimer's disease. The Alzheimer's Association provides resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, including educational programs, virtual events, and an online community for support. They also offer research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research, as well as opportunities for advocacy and philanthropy. The organization partners with various corporations and sponsors to support their mission.
