The Alzheimer's Association has appointed Dr. Joanne Pike as its new CEO. The organization provides resources and support for individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers, including information on diagnosis, treatments, and clinical trials. They also offer educational programs, virtual events, and an online community for support, as well as resources specific to different communities. The Alzheimer's Association emphasizes the importance of brain health and offers research, clinical trials, and funding opportunities for international dementia research. They also partner with corporations and sponsors to support their mission.
