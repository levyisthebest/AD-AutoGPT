The "sandwich generation" of caregivers, who care for elderly relatives with dementia and raise children, need more support, especially BIPOC caregivers who face additional challenges due to institutional and structural racism. Many caregivers are unpaid family members and lack healthcare access, knowledge, and face stigma. Juggling paid employment and caregiving duties can lead to financial strain and mental health concerns. The impact of dementia on the family unit, including children, can be significant.
