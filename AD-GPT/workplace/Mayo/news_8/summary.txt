Mayo Clinic provides advice on managing sleep problems in individuals with Alzheimer's disease, including promoting a routine, avoiding stimulants, encouraging physical activity, and managing medications. Melatonin and bright light therapy may also be helpful. Caregivers should prioritize their own sleep and seek help if needed.
