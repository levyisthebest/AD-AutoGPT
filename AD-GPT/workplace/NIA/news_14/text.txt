Join NIA at On Aging 2023
March 07, 2023
Connect with NIA staff at On Aging 2023 from March 27-30, 2023, in Atlanta. This annual conference is hosted by the American Society on Aging. During the meeting you can stop by NIA’s exhibit booth (#411) to meet NIA staff, explore new print publications, and learn what’s new at the institute. The exhibit hall is open Sunday through Wednesday at the following times:
Sunday, March 26: 6:00 p.m. - 7:30 p.m., ET
Monday, March 27: 11 a.m. - 3:30 p.m., ET
Tuesday, March 28: 12:45 p.m. - 3:45 p.m., ET
Wednesday, March 29: 12:00 p.m. – 3:30 p.m., ET
We also want to hear from you! Are you funded by NIA? Do you use our materials regularly in your work? Stop by to take a photo with NIA’s booth and share it on social media. Tag NIA on Twitter (@NIHAging) or LinkedIn (National Institute on Aging) and use the hashtags #NIAfunded or #NIHAging in your post.
