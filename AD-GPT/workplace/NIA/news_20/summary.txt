The National Institute on Aging is hosting an #AskNIA event on Twitter on January 25, 2023, where early career researchers can ask questions about applying for their first NIA research award. The event will feature NIA's training and career development experts. The National Institutes of Health website offers accessibility support, FOIA requests, performance reports, and policies and notices, as well as social media accounts and a newsletter for updates and resources. The website also includes information on the Office of the Inspector General and the Vulnerability Disclosure Policy.
