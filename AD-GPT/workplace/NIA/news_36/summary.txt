The National Institute on Aging is sponsoring a virtual meeting on December 2, 2022, to provide information on research and training opportunities for those new to aging research and underrepresented in the field. The meeting will include breakout sessions for participants to ask questions related to NIA's research divisions, programs, and training. Researchers interested in health disparities research are encouraged to attend, and there is no registration fee. The National Institutes of Health website offers resources on accessibility support, FOIA requests, performance reports, policies and notices, the No FEAR Act data, Office of the Inspector General, and vulnerability disclosure policy.
