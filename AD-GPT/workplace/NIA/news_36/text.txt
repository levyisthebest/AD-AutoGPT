NIA Director’s Regional Meeting 2022
December 02, 2022
NIA will be sponsoring a virtual Director’s Regional Meeting on Dec. 2, 2022, from 2:30-6 p.m. Eastern Standard Time, EST (9:30 a.m.-1 p.m. Hawaii Standard Time, HST). We invite participants from your institution and departments to our half-day meeting on research and training for individuals new to aging research as well as those underrepresented in aging research.
The program is offered by NIA’s Office of Special Populations, and researchers with an interest in health disparities research are encouraged to attend. Applicants from diverse backgrounds, including individuals from underrepresented racial and ethnic groups, individuals with disabilities, and women are always encouraged to apply for NIH support.
NIA speakers
Richard Hodes, M.D., NIA Director
Patricia Jones, Dr.P.H., M.P.H., M.S., MBA, Director, Office of Special Populations
Kenneth Santora, Ph.D., Director, Division of Extramural Activities
Regional Meeting goals
Provide information on existing opportunities for research and training.
Provide hands-on technical assistance in grant writing.
Solicit advice on the design of new research opportunities.
Gain strategies for recruiting underrepresented students and investigators to aging research.
Registration
There is no registration fee for the 2022 NIA Directors Regional Meeting on Aging Research. Please register at the Director’s Regional Meeting 2022 form.
Access the full meeting agenda.
There will be two breakout sessions on the agenda for participants to ask specific questions related to NIA’s research divisions and programs, training, and small business innovation research and small business technology transfer programs.
