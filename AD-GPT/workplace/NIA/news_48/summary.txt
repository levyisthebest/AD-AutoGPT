The National Institute on Aging will hold a virtual workshop in October 2022 to explore the relationship between Alzheimer's disease and cancer, with the goal of identifying research gaps and opportunities and determining possible future directions for research. The workshop will discuss topics such as the link between cancer and Alzheimer's disease, genes, mechanisms, and epidemiological evidence, cancer chemotherapy and cognitive dysfunction, and drug repurposing.
