The National Institute on Aging has released a budget proposal for FY2024 outlining funds needed for research opportunities in Alzheimer's Disease and Related Dementias, with an emphasis on health equity, inclusion, and workforce diversity. A scientific progress report will be released separately this fall. The National Institutes of Health website offers updates and resources on accessibility support, FOIA requests, performance reports, policies and notices, No FEAR Act data, Office of the Inspector General, and vulnerability disclosure policy.
