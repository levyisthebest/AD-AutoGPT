An 83-year-old man from Swindon has spoken about his experience caring for his wife with Alzheimer's for the past 12 years. He has given his support to a campaign by the Alzheimer's Society and thanked the Swindon Dementia Support Team for their help. The man has been praised for his dedication to caring for his wife, having given up his job to do so. Their story highlights the challenges faced by carers of loved ones with dementia and the need for better support and resources.
