Ruby, a 12-year-old girl from Merthyr Tydfil, discusses how her father's death and great-grandmother's dementia affected her mental health. She participated in the Youth Sport Trust's active in mind programme, which helped her to discuss her mental health and develop good habits to nurture a healthy mind. The CEO of the Youth Sport Trust encourages young people to share their emotions and not be afraid to cry. The article also highlights the impact of the cost of living emergency on young people's mental health.
