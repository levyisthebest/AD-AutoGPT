An 85-year-old care home resident in Derbyshire with Alzheimer's and Parkinson's disease has returned to playing football after staff at Milford House helped him fulfill his wish. Geoff Mansfield attends a walking football group at Belper Town Football Club and has seen an improvement in his physical and mental wellbeing. The home has organised for him to attend sessions every week. The BBC provides information on their policies, cookies, accessibility, parental guidance, and how to make an editorial complaint.
