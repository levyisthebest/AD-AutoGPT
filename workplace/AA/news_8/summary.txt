The Part the Cloud luncheon, in partnership with the Alzheimer's Association, raised $67 million to fund 65 research projects in Alzheimer's and dementia research, which have gone on to receive over $1.2 billion in follow-on funding. The event featured a panel of experts and was attended by over 200 people. Alzheimer's disease affects over 6 million Americans over age 65 and is estimated to cost the nation $345 billion in 2023.
