Dr. Marie A. Bernard has been appointed as the Chief Officer for Scientific Workforce Diversity at the National Institutes of Health (NIH) to lead efforts to promote diversity, inclusiveness, and equity in biomedical research. She has been serving as the acting COSWD since October 2020 and has played key leadership roles in various NIH activities to further diversity. Dr. Bernard is an accomplished physician-scientist and has championed diversity and inclusion throughout her career.
